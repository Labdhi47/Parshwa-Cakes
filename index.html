Frequently Asked Questions

How many days in advance should I order?

For simple birthday cakes, please order 1–2 days in advance. For wedding or highly detailed custom cakes, 2–3 days is recommended.

Do you offer delivery?

No, we don't offer delivery

Can you customize the design and flavors?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs. You can share your inspiration images with us, and we'll work with you to create the perfect cake in your favorite flavor.

Do you provide cake toppers and candles?

Basic candles are included. Custom acrylic or wooden cake toppers can be arranged for an additional charge if requested in advance.

What are your payment terms?

To confirm your order, we require a 50% advance payment. The remaining balance can be paid upon delivery or pickup via UPI or Cash.

Do you make wheat cake or maida cake or both?

We make both wheat cakes and maida cakes based on your preference.

Do you make sugarfree cake?

Yes, we do make sugar-free cakes on request.
These cakes are specially prepared using sugar substitutes, making them suitable for health-conscious customers and diabetic-friendly diets.

Kindly inform us in advance while placing the order.

Do you take any advance payment?

Yes, we take a partial advance payment to confirm your order.
Advance payment helps us secure your booking and prepare your cake fresh according to your customization.

The remaining amount can be paid at the time of delivery/pickup.

Do you have bakery?

We do not have a physical bakery store.
We operate as a home-based cake business, where all cakes are freshly baked with proper hygiene and quality care.

Orders are taken on a pre-booking basis only.
